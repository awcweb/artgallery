AWC art students have been touring Los Angeles’ world class museums and galleries since 2004.

“LA Trip” participants visit the potpourri of galleries that is Bergamot Station; the mountaintop Getty Center and Villa; the Broad downtown and the sprawling LA County Museum of Art. The group lodges for three days near the beach in Santa Monica’s architecturally and culturally remarkable Main Street and Ocean Park Blvd neighborhood, the stomping grounds of iconic 20th century American artists and iconic figures.
