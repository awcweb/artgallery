Project History
Dialogos E Interpretaciones was established in 2012 with the goal of creating greater artistic and cultural exchange between printmakers of the United States and Colombia.

The 2013 exchange was expanded to North and South America. 2014 and 2015 we invited printmaking artists from around the globe to participate.

In 2016 we organized a special invitational portfolio for our 5 year Anniversary.
Organized by Jules Floss, Arizon Western College, South County, Yuma , Arizona
and Jorge Eliécer Rodríguez Osorio, Universidad de Caldas, Manizales
